Mission Writing Guide
Works Best
  • Drive forward until a door or doorway is clearly visible, then stop immediately.
  • Move forward down the corridor until you are facing the doorway ahead, then stop.
  • Turn right until the open doorway is centered in the camera, then stop.
  • Drive forward until you see a chair, then stop.
Avoid These
  • Go to the next room.
  • Move a little further and stop near it.
  • Take the second left after the first door.
  • Find the best path and stop at the correct place.
Full Drive works best with one visible goal and one clear stop condition. It follows the dashboard Auto Speed setting while it is driving.
